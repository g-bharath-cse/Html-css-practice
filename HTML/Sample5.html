INTRODUCTION OF AMAZON 1

Amazon is a multinational technology company founded by Jeff Bezos in 1994, initially as an online bookstore. It has since evolved into a diverse conglomerate with significant market presence in various industries.

INTRODUCTION OF AMAZON 2

E-commerce: Amazon is the world's largest online retailer and marketplace. It offers a vast selection of products ranging from electronics and clothing to groceries and books, sold through its own platform (Amazon.com) and by third-party sellers who utilize Amazon's infrastructure.

INTRODUCTION OF AMAZON 3

Cloud Computing: Amazon Web Services (AWS) provides on-demand cloud computing platforms and APIs, enabling businesses and individuals to access computing power, storage, and other services on a pay-as-you-go basis. AWS is a dominant force in the cloud market.

INTRODUCTION OF AMAZON 4

Digital Streaming & Entertainment: Amazon offers various digital streaming services, including Amazon Prime Video (movies and TV shows), Amazon Music (music and podcasts), and Twitch (live-streaming, especially gaming).

INTRODUCTION OF AMAZON 5

Consumer Electronics: Amazon develops and sells popular electronic devices like the Kindle e-reader, Fire tablets and TVs, and Echo smart speakers powered by the Alexa voice assistant.

INTRODUCTION OF AMAZON 6

Amazon's success is often attributed to its customer focus, innovative strategies, and diversified business model. However, the company has also faced criticism regarding its business practices, including issues related to labor conditions, environmental impact, and market dominance.
